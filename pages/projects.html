MY PROJECTS

This is a collection of the projects I have worked on throughout my coding journey so far.

I invite you to explore each project below and view their GitHub repositories.

  • what the weather

    JAVASCRIPT / NODE.JS / EXPRESS

    What The Weather is built for those who struggle to associate the temperature outside to the type of clothing they should wear. What The Weather makes use of the OpenWeatherMap api to get a 5-day forecast for the user's location, and then translates it into clothing recommendations. Users can also search for a specific city to get the weather forecast for their location. Find out if today is sweater weather in a girlypop way!

  • ServiceHub

    JAVASCRIPT / NODE.JS / MYSQL / EXPRESS

    ServiceHub is a full-stack web-based platform designed for service-oriented businesses to manage their services and streamline client interactions. It provides an intuitive interface for companies to customize their listings, handle bookings, and track client requests, while administrators can manage business settings, services, and billing. Clients can browse available services, book appointments, and track their service history through a user-friendly dashboard. ServiceHub functions as a flexible template for businesses to establish an online presence and efficiently manage their operations.

  • FreeMET

    JAVASCRIPT / NODE.JS / EXPRESS / EJS

    Save the trip to NYC and visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art from the comfort of your home! FreeMET brings a virtual museum experience by randomly showcasing art from the MET. Throught the click of a button, users can view a random piece of art from the MET's collection, along with its title, artist, year, and some extra details on the piece. FreeMET is a fun and educational way to explore the MET's vast collection and discover new art pieces.

  • Tic-Tac-Toe

    JAVA

    Enjoy a relaxing game of Tic-Tac-Toe against a bot built in Java and playable on the terminal. The bot is programmed to make a random move in the available sqaures each turn, and the game continues until a player wins or the board is full.
